BIOGRAPHY
JIMCARREY is an award-winning actor who has been honored forboth his dramatic and comedic work. In 1999, he won a Golden GlobeAward, for Best Actor in a Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy, for thetitle role in Peter Weir's "TheTruman Show." The following year he won his second GoldenGlobe, for Best Actor in a Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy, for hisportrayal of comedian Andy Kaufman in Milos Forman's "Manon the Moon." Over the course of his career, Carrey has alsobeen recognized numerous times by the MTV Movie Awards, People's ChoiceAwards, and Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards.

GOSSIP, NEWS & TRIVIA
Do you have any information about Jim Carrey to share?

  • Became a grandfather at 47 after his daughter Jane Carrey, 22, gave birth to a baby boy. (February 26, 2010)
  • Hired a skywriter to declare his love for Jenny McCarthy on Valentine's Day. (February 14, 2010)
  • Jim Carrey and Ewan McGregor shared a kiss in Paris, France after they were made knights in the prestigious National Order of Arts and Letters at the Ministry of Culture. During the knighthood session, French Culture Minister Frederic Mitterrand proudly proclaimed, I love you Jim Carrey! I love you Ewan McGregor! in reference to I LOVE YOU PHILLIP MORRIS, the film produced by Luc Besson and in which Carrey and McGregor play two prison inmates who fall for each other after sharing a cell together. (February 1, 2010)
  • Loved Christmas time when he was growing up because it gave him the audience he needed to practice his comedy and would do anything to make them laugh.
  • Walked out on his role in the highly anticipated screen adaptation of The Three Stooges, even though he had gone as far as packing on the pounds in order to better portray the chubby Curly. (August 2009)
  • Ranked #8 on Forbes' list of Hollywood's Top Earning Actors ($27.8 million) in 2009
  • MTV Movie Awards: Best Comedic Performance, YES MAN (2009)
  • Loves Bikram yoga – despite having never tried it himself - because he can't get enough of his girlfriend Jenny McCarthy's toned derriere.
  • Is in talks to play Curly in THE THREE STOOGES, a comedy based on the classic 1930s vaudeville trio. (March 2009)
  • Has cast in an updated, big-screen musical version of “Damn Yankees.” (February 2009)
  • Joked that kissing Ewan McGregor in I LOVE YOU PHILLIP MORRIS was a ''dream come true.''
  • Was serenaded by 50 Cent on his 47th birthday. The actor and his girlfriend Jenny McCarthy were watching the rapper perform when 50 spotted them in the crowd and asked Jim to join him on stage in Park City, Utah. (January 17, 2009)
  • Sexily dances for partner Jenny McCarthy over the internet when they are apart.
  • Arrived at the London premiere of his new film YES MAN in a double-decker bus. (December 9, 2008)
