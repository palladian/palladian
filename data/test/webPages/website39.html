This is a list of mobile phones that have shipped or sold at least one million units.

  • Nokia 1100 (200 million)[1]
  • Nokia 3310 (126 million)[2]
  • Motorola RAZR V3 (100 million shipped)[3]
  • Nokia 2100 (20 million)[2]
  • LG Chocolate (15 million)[4]
  • BlackBerry Pearl (10 million)[5]
  • Samsung SGH-E700 (10 million)[6]
  • iPhone (8 million in the U.S.)[7]
  • LG Shine (8 million)[4]
  • Nokia N95 (7 million)[8]
  • Motorola MING A1200i (3 million in China only)[9]
  • N-Gage (3 million)[10]
  • Sony Ericsson W300 (3 million)[11]
  • HTC Touch (2 million)[12]
  • LG Viewty (KU990) (2 million)[13]
  • Nokia 6600 (2 million shipped)[14]
  • Palm Centro (2 million)[15]
  • LG Venus (VX8800) (1.6 million)[13]
  • LG Voyager (VX10000) (1.3 million)[13]
  • iPhone 3G (1 million)[16]
  • Motorola Q (1 million in U.S.)[17]
  • Nokia 3250 (1 million)[18]
  • Nokia 8800 (1 million)[19]
  • Samsung U900 Soul (1 million)[20]
