Product Information

SigLiteSL USB (T-SL460-USB)

SigLite SL is Topaz's slim low-cost electronic signature pad. SigLite SL features all the high-quality biometric and forensic capture techniques of a SigLite tablet but with a thin 0.20' profile for extreme portability. The touchpad sensor is equipped with a protective cover when not in use. Provided with a USB connector.

The touchpad sensor is protected by a replaceable overlay for longer life.

SigLite LCD is Topaz's low-cost pressure-sensitive electronic signature capture pad. SigLite features all the high-quality biometric and forensic capture techniques of a SignatureGem LCD tablet but with a low-cost touchpad and stylus in place of the more rugged digitizing pen and inert tempered glass surface of the SigGem tablet series.


The SigLite SL signature capture touchpad tablet are designed to provide the benefits of electronic signatures in an lower-volume environment where cost and size are significant issues. The SigLite SL is extremely portable, with a profile of only 0.20” and large 5” signing area. To protect the touchpad surface during transit or when not in use, SigLite SL features a hard, thin platic sensor cover that attaches easily to the tablet face. SigLite boasts the additional value of powerful bundled software tools and support, including ActiveX, Java, Wintab drivers, and MS Office and Adobde Acrobat plugins for the capture, encryption, formatting, and secure processing of signatures.


Features



SigPlus software is compliant with eSign, state and federal digital signature regulations by virtue of its ability to bind and encrypt the signature to a document or electronic form data. Examples and software plug-ins are bundled with the tablet.

  • Serial Port-Powered, USB option available
  • Small, Lightweight and Portable, only 6 ounces, 0.20” thick
  • High-Speed Data Conversion, 377 points per second for accurate signatures
  • Replaceable Mylar Sensor Overlay and hard sensor cover, increases tablet life by protecting touchpad surface
  • Powerful Bundled Software and Support, ActiveX, Java, MS Office, and Adobe Acrobat plugins at no additonal charge
  • E-Sign law-compliant biometric signatures and software, encryption, compression, and authentication for eCommerce
