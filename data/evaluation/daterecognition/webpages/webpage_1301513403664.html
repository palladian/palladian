Nectar Announces Support of Avaya Aura(TM) Session Manager
Monday February 28, 2011 - 10:09 AM EST
Marketwire News Releases
Released By Nectar Services Corp
CMP Vendor Knowledge Module (VKM) Introduced to Support Avaya Aura(TM) Session Manager
Share:
Text Size:
 
 

ORLANDO, FL -- (Marketwire) -- 02/28/11 -- Nectar Services Corp. announced at Enterprise Connect that it has extended support of its market leading Converged Management Platform (CMP) to include the Avaya AuraÂ™ Session Manager 6.0 monitoring module. Nectar is a software firm specializing in managed services technologies for voice and data networks, and is a wholly owned subsidiary of Juma Technology Corp (OTCBB: JUMT).

Nectar CMP is an intelligent network management platform that incorporates monitoring and management at all layers of the network from the premises into the service provider network to provide a holistic view of the health of the enterprise network. CMP enhances support of global enterprise networks by providing real-time, service affecting information in a clear and easily accessible manner to the global service provider, the local response team, and the enterprise customer simultaneously to apprise all parties immediately of problems affecting the enterprise business anywhere in the world.

With the expansion of Nectar's platform support to include Avaya's Aura Session Manager, customers are now able to:

  • Determine the viability of Session Manager links between Avaya and non-Avaya SIP entities
  • Support SIP traces from Session Manager
  • Review real-time RTCP session statistics for SIP endpoints registered to Session Manger

"The release of Nectar's VKM for Aura Session Manager again demonstrates our ongoing effort to expand CMP to include support of an ever increasing number of network elements," said Edmond Baydian, Chief Services Officer, Nectar.

Other recent announcements of VKMs to support additional network elements include:

  • Avaya Voice Portal -- announced November 2010
  • Avaya IP Office Systems -- announced April 2010

"Nectar's continued focus on expanding features and network element support to the Converged Management Platform underscores our commitment to provide our customers with the tools they need to manage their increasingly complex global networks," said Anthony Servidio, CEO, Nectar. "Nectar is dedicated to helping enterprises and their service providers improve network performance through the use of real-time, accurate and relevant network information when and where they need it."

The Avaya Aura Session Manager module for CMP is critical to Nectar's customers and partners as Session Manager is the central hub of Avaya's enterprise telephony architecture. Nectar's ability to monitor and troubleshoot advanced SIP networks that incorporate Session Manager along with CM, AVP, CMS, AES, MM, Call Recording, Conferencing, data networks is unmatched in the industry and reinforces Nectar's commitment to lead the market.

About Nectar Services Corp (www.nectarcorp.com)
Nectar Services Corp. is an IP communications software firm specializing in managed services for voice and data networks. Nectar, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Juma Technology Corp (OTCBB: JUMT), maintains a suite of service platforms: the Converged Management Platform (CMP) and Enterprise Session Management (ESM). The Nectar offerings preserve investments in existing telecommunications systems while transitioning to IP, and provide advanced monitoring and management for complex networks. These innovative solutions deliver significant cost savings, inherent business continuity, intelligent call routing and the centralization of both applications and management. Follow us on Twitter: www.twitter.com/nectarcorp.
