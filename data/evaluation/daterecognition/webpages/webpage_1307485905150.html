Lots of items on sale just in time for Mother's Day

Posted: Tuesday, May. 03, 2011

Here’s an update to get you caught up on the deals in Sunday, Monday and Tuesday papers:

Shopko’s Tuesday insert** touts its Wednesday only sale with plenty of gift ideas for mom, including the entire stock of women’s sweaters for 50 percent off and the entire stock of fine jewelry is 60 percent off.

Wrapped around Tuesday’s Life section are about 20 coupons for discounts at local businesses, including $8.99 for a basic facial at the Milan Institute.

Macy’s ad in Tuesday’s paper features its Mother’s Day sale. Plus, there’s that extra savings pass where you can get an extra 15 percent off select sale and clearance apparel. There are lots of beautiful watches and other jewelry items on sale that would make great gifts for mom.

Looking for new siding? Champion siding is 33 percent off right now. Champion windows and patio rooms are also on sale. See the ad on page A9 of Monday’s paper for more information.

There’s an ad on the front of Monday’s Life section where Gold’s Gym is offering a free trial membership when you sign up for a free personal assessment with a Gold’s personal trainer.

In Sunday’s paper, you’ll find your guide to the 2011 Parade of Homes. If you are house shopping, taking the time to see some of the homes on this tour is a great way to get ideas and find out what’s new in the market. The Parade of Homes takes place through May 15.

We love to travel to Oregon and the Oregon Coast! In Sunday’s paper, there was an insert with lots of travel and recreation information. Plus, there’s a coupon to request travel brochures.

Did you grab your Albertsons insert out of Sunday’s paper? Don’t forget to use your Twice the Value Coupons by Tuesday (tonight!).

There were five (5!!!!) manufacturers coupon inserts in Sunday’s Idaho Statesman that included high-value coupons for pet food, laundry detergent, chocolate, Frito-Lay products, Proctor and Gamble products and more.

In the Parade Magazine, there’s a $10 off coupon for your Teleflora.com purchase — just in time for Mother’s Day shopping.

Toys R Us has 25 percent off all Schwinn bikes and helmets.

FarWest Landscape/Garden Center has coupons in Sunday’s paper for 20 percent off any gift shop item and 20 percent off hanging baskets. There are also coupons for many other products. And don’t miss the store’s list of free gardening classes.

Walmart’s insert is advertising $10 Mother’s Day flower bouquets and many other fun gift ideas for mom. Plus, the ad features a ton of summer items — camping equipment, lawn-care items, bikes and more.

Fred Meyer’s ad features sweet corn at 6 for $2, 5 for $5 avocados, 5 for $5 organic mangos and more. There are also several great food coupons — and it’s double points week! Check out the flower specials, too -- $9.99 for a dozen roses and many more.

The Sunday paper also had the Better Business Bureau’s directory in it. It’s a guide to “BBB accredited businesses in the Snake River Region.” This might be a handy guide to keep around so that you have some basic information when making big repairs, remodeling or the like.

**Remember, advertisers can choose to zone their inserts by delivery area, so the exact inserts that you receive will vary by where you live in the Treasure Valley. Also, the above items are just some of the advertising highlights from your Idaho Statesman. See your paper for many more discounts and values.
