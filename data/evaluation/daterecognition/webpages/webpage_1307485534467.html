Power Performance © Affiliate Program Overview.

Earn Money With Our Power Performance © Products.

We're looking for a few good web-sites. If you manage or own a web-site, we'd like to invite you to join The Power Performance Affiliate Program. By partnering with us, you'll immediately start earning commissions on every Power Performance © product purchased through your site.

Why should you become a Power Performance © Affiliate?
  • Earn extra income: Our Power Performance products offers something for just about everybody - including many of your web-sites own readers, visitors and customers. By joining the Power Performance Affiliate program and letting telling them know about our products, you can earn some extra money.
  • Offer added value to your visitors: By including our motivational and inspirational articles, stories and other material on your web-site, you'll offer your visitors additional value. This will enhance the impression you'll make on your visitors and customers. Many affiliates will notice that this also increases the sales of their own products and services!
  • Receive exceptional support and guidance: When you join our affiliate program, you become part of our "team." Your success is our success, so we'll do our best to help you succeed in our program.
  • Our program is free to join: You can immediately begin generating an ongoing stream of income without any cost or obligation. You simply place a link to us on your site (text link or banner link) or mention us in a newsletter or email. You have nothing to lose. If you don't find the association to be as profitable as you would like, just remove the links to our site. It's that simple. But we're sure you'll find it well worth your while!
We pay out huge commissions to our affiliates.
For every sale generated by visitors coming from your link-now or in the future-we pay you a 35% first tier commission and a 5% second tier commission. Please note that all prices and commissions are in US dollars.

How do you earn commissions?
To earn commissions as an affiliate, just promote our products using your special linking code. Each time you refer a prospect (when they click on your link), we'll track that prospect with a variety of tracking mechanisms as your client. When your referral purchases a product, your account is credited with the commission from the sale. When you're an affiliate, we'll reward you for every purchase that a user makes from your site. We use the CFWebStore software to track and report on visitors and sales.

At any time, you can log in to your secured administration area and see real time statistics.

We'll do all of the work.
  • We take the orders on our web site using our proven software and servers.
  • We handle all customer questions and requests, including cancellations and returns.
  • We process all payments and orders.
  • You simply link to us and you get paid for everything sold from your links to our site.
Ready to join? It's as easy as 1, 2, 3.
  1. Read our Affiliate Agreement carefully.
  2. Fill out the Application form and you'll be assigned a special URL which you can link to (so we can track your orders). Click here to sign up as an affiliate.
  3. Within your personal Affiliate Admin area you can create a link from your site to our web-site. You can place text links or banner links anywhere on your site you deem appropriate. Or you mention us in your newsletter, communications to your mailing list, etc. Then repeat as desired; you're in charge!
You can decide how you want to incorporate the Power Performance © link(s).
Here are some linking ideas (the more you use - the more opportunities you have to make money):
  • You might add a text link to Power Performance © from your home page - or all of your web pages.
  • If your site includes articles, reviews or interviews, you might prefer to simply write a review on Success Net and its publications and let people click on a link that will take them to our site.
  • We have a large number of articles available to you for reprint on your site. You get top quality content to display on your site and may use your referral link in the article so that anyone visiting our site as a result of the article gets "tagged" with your ID. If they buy, you get paid.
  • You may wish to have our banner on your site which will link them to us. We've developed several banners (and are creating more) you can use to link to our site.
Upon joining you will receive your Affiliate ID and access to The Power Performance marketing materials that you can place on your web-site, send to your email list, use in your signature file, or use in any promotion or campaign. If you'd like to discuss any promotional ideas, or if you require some custom creative work, just let us know by sending an email to Power Performance © Support.

When you apply to participate, we send you an email response which includes instructions on how to link to our site. As soon as you receive email notification of your account being approved (which is sent to you within a few minutes), and the instructions for linking to us, you can start displaying the link(s) to our web site.

Once your application has been approved, you'll automatically begin earning referral fees for sales generated by these links. You'll receive referral-fee checks and online, real-time access to statements.

If you have any questions prior to signing up, check out our Frequently Asked Questions page or feel free to contact us.
