Reasons to Choose Calgary Car Hire


  • One of western Canada's biggest and youngest cities, Calgary lies in the south-central area of Alberta state.
  • Calgary is a stone’s throw away from the spectacular Canadian Rockies, as well as Elbow and Bow Rivers.
  • Predominantly flat and easy to drive around, the city of Calgary is split into four distinguishable areas: Calgary's northwest, northeast, southwest and southeast districts.
  • From winter to summer, the climate in Calgary can be extreme. This is all because of the Pacific Ocean being located hundreds of miles away to the west.
  • In terms of views and natural scenery, Calgary won’t disappoint. Come winter, snow fills the region to make it a winter paradise. For even more experiences of pleasure, the natural park of Banff is within driving distance from Calgary if you head northwest.
  • The 1988 Winter Olympics were held in Calgary.

Car Hire Calgary: Top Tourist Information
