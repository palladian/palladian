Affiliate Product Launches and Products

Welcome to my rant

I want to give everyone fair warning. This is going to be negative. There is going to be foul language. So, if you are one of those people that can only read positive things, or blushes at the sound or sight of profanity…leave now…I’m serious. This is a poorly thought out and probably poorly executed rant so, read at your own risk.

/begin affiliate career suicide

When I started Internet Marketing a little over 2 years ago, my main mission and goal was to set up a bunch of little niche sites, write a bunch of articles, and make a bunch of money. My business plan was something like this:

1. Find offer
2. Find Keywords
3. Write till my fingers hurt
4. ???
5. ???
6 Profit

Pretty sweet business plan…huh? Well, surprisingly the shit worked…and worked VERY WELL. It wasn’t long before I was making a couple extra G’s a month, and life was good. So, like anyone excited in Internet Marketing, I ran to a forum and made a post about what I was doing, and how much money I was making. I’ll be damned if that thread didn’t get like 50 pages long, and I didn’t get over 100 PM’s from people asking me how I did it.

I also got some PM’s from a few product creators that said: “dude, you would be nuts not to make a product about this” So, I did… and I sold over 700 copies of that product at $22 a piece…good times…good times

After that, I met up with Don, and we started doing all kinds of cool shit from CPA, to even starting up an Offline company a little over a year ago, so the Internet has definitely been good for me, but probably the last 6 months has really made me rethink which direction that I want to go.

You see, after selling my first product, I was all like: “how cool would it be to make products and have some of these big affiliates promote it..Man, I would bank so hard!” And while I was doing my niche marketing, and doing my CPA stuff, and running an offline company that same thought was always in the back of my mind.

THEN I STARTED SEEING THINGS FOR WHAT THEY WERE…

When Don and I make a product we do it for a couple reasons. One of them being that it is a good change of pace from the normal stuff we do, the other is that it gives a nice cash boost for things like buying my 17 year old a car, or for Don going on vacation for a month. It’s just fun money if you know what I mean. We definitely like the money…but, we’re not out to break the bank or anything, or to take someones last dollar.

But, when I took a long hard look at the affiliate “circle” that I once wanted to be a part of, to be honest…I was a little bit disgusted. Don’t get me wrong, there are tons of affiliates, and product creator out there that are HONEST and actually market with INTEGRITY, but when you really look at the guys that are very visible…the ones that kind of define affiliate marketing…the ones that everyone knows about… the ones hammering out product after product…well, it’s a different story.

I’m talking about the “new breed” because in all honesty, I would probably still shell out a couple grand for a Frank Kern product because the ones that I’ve seen actually provided value to me…they actually gave me some legitimate insight, and to me at least were worth the money I paid. The new breed though cranks out high ticket – low value products weekly it seems like..and the worst part is if you actually read the products, it doesn’t take you long to figure out that they have no clue what the fuck they are talking about. They are doing nothing but shooting fish in a barrel..and you are likely one of those fish.

So, let’s talk about a couple of big launch patterns lately that were being sold for at least a COUPLE HUNDRED DOLLARS.

A few of them were using the angle of telling all of us dumb asses about some secret site that gets gazillions of visitors a month and offers laser targeted
traffic…Hell, according to them, just knowing about this site would put enough money in your pocket that you could wipe your ass with hundred dollar bills. Now, guess what the secret site was…go ahead…guess. Still don’t know? IT WAS FACEBOOK! Holy shit, these guys really do think we are stupid, and hell, we might be stupid. After all, we just paid a couple hundred to a couple thousand dollars, in some cases to someone that has probably never spent a SINGLE DOLLAR advertising on FaceBook. I mean, come on..It would be one thing if they showed how to use the advertising platform, how to target demographics, and how to bank, but for the most part, they were just letting us know about the sites existence.

Then you had the barrage of products that aimed to fill us all in on the super secret ways to get traffic from search engines. According to the sales letters, and all of the idiots spamming Googles index with with review pages for these products..the were the end all of all traffic products! Shit, the secrets that they were given away would get you more traffic than the free needle van in the ghetto…That’s a shit load of traffic right. Well, my dumb ass decided…what the hell, I’ll go ahead and grab a couple of these to see what’s up. I mean, if I can get more traffic than the free needle van, I could do some serious damage, right?

So, I read em’, watch the videos, and than call the police immediately because I realize I’ve been penetrated! Not only did I not find any secrets, but the secrets that they thought they were sharing have been in ebooks sold on DP that were stolen from people on the warrior forum who stole them from blackhat world who stole them from wickedfire, who stole them from pedo bear at ASE. I mean, the circle jerk involved here is HUGE, even days later, I’m still trying to figure out who the pivot man is.

Don’t get me wrong, there are some excellent products out there, so don’t think I’m hating on all information products, because I’m not. I’m specifically talking about the “launch group” whose only intention is to cash in on shit that they have no idea about. It’s gotten to the point where they don’t even care if you know that they don’t know anything about it.

Here’s an example, and a somewhat true story.

Guru A makes a product and in his launch material and sales copy says that Twitter sucks, and only losers use Twitter. He then sells an ungodly number of his anti-twitter product – KA CHING! Then…2 weeks later proclaims that he is a Twitter expert and he wants to show you how to dominate twitter…because everyone knows that If you don’t know Twitter…you don’t know shit. Wait…wut?

At this point you would think that the B-Team (his/her affiliates) would be like…”dude, we can’t promote this crap…just last week, you said twitter sucked!) But, did they do that? Hell no…they promoted that shit like Jesus Christ himself took the time to make the product, and that just by reading it you would have access to 21 virgins…it was a real religious experience.

The bottom line is, they have the buyers (you?) trained so well that you will buy whatever the hell it is that they want to sell you. Nothing personal…It’s just business, right?…and while you’re at it, make sure that you buy the 27 up and down sells…which will without a doubt consist of:

21 offers for a “lost chapter” which they will without shame tell you that without it their product won’t work
3 offers for software that won’t be done for another 6 months
…and 3 offers for a forum that you won’t be able to access for 2 months which will promptly be deleted as soon as people start to complain that they aren’t making any money

No big deal anyway though, because according to their next sales letter that product will suck anyway…the next product, and the next 27 up and down sells is exactly what you need. Cool, huh?

Listen, I know that this is a business, and selling information products IS A LEGITIMATE BUSINESS MODEL. However, Being a douche bag while you do it, being a lying sack of shit, and being a parasite should be grounds for getting your ass kicked.

BUT

You would have one hell of a time getting to any of these guys to kick their ass…Their “b-teams” would lay down their lives, ruin their own reputations, and do whatever necessary to keep your grubby hands, the truth, or reality far far away from their “gurus”. You see, in some respects, the B-Team is probably worse than the “bad” product creators. Typically, the B-Team are people you trust, maybe someone you picked up some good tips from. These are the guys that fill your inboxes up with email after email at launch time…these are the guys that are “setting you up” by saying things like…

“I’ve known XXX for a long time and he’s the real deal”

“XXX showed me what he was doing, and I was actually shocked”

“XXX helped me take me triple my revenue, and he will help you too”

Most of the time, they don’t know the guru, the guru has never shown them shit, and the only thing that the guru has ever given them is a series of shitty emails to fill your inbox up with to get you to a squeeze page so that you can get on the gurus list…so that they can get a commission. And they will continue this process and repeat it over and over again until you opt out of their list.

I’m going to wrap this rant up now, but understand this…Internet Marketing is filled with predators. Sure, they want you to think that they are there to help you…blah blah blah…but, their number one goal is to get your money at any and all costs. Stop being an idiot…the next time someone tries to sell you an info product for a couple hundred or a couple thousand dollars that is filled with nothing but interviews from 4 years ago, and tells you go to FaceBook and get rich, or start a blog and you will be a millionaire..tell them to kiss your ass, and then punch then kick them in the nuts if you are close
enough to them to get a shot in.

It’s also filled with a lot of good and genuine people, but you have to use common sense to separate the good guys from the bad guys…nobody can really do that for you.

/rant
