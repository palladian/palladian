Model RBI/SBI Floor to Floor Incline Conveyor (Roller Bed/Slider Bed)
 Model RBI: View Catalog Sheet  Download Catalog Sheet
Model SBI: View Catalog Sheet  Download Catalog Sheet

The Model RBI & SBI are floor-to-floor incline conveyors. They are equipped with a chain driven power feeder and an adjustable double nose-over at the discharge end to insure a smooth transfer from incline to horizontal plane. The RBI conveyor, with rollers, makes higher load capacities possible. The SBI has full width galvanized pans. Inclines are easily adjusted up to 30�. These conveyors can also be used as a booster conveyor in gravity flow systems.

  • Motor - 3/4 HP-2 HP with brake kit based on load capacity
  • Reversible
  • Center Drive (standard)
  • Capacity can range up to 1245 lbs.
